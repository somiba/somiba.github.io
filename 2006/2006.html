2006-07 Season Dates:

 

·          October 18, 2006 – preseason began

·          October 27, 2006 – draft lottery was held on the league’s message board

·          October 29, 2006 – expansion draft for new league members

·          November 1, 2006 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – each team cut down to 10 players

·          November 4, 2006 at 8:00am Pacific Time – rookie/free-agent draft

·          November 7, 2006 at 10:30pm Pacific Time – one waiver wire claim was processed

·          November 13, 2006 at 9:00pm PST – trade deadline and games missed list deadline

·          November 18, 2006 – start of the season

·          December 9, 2006 – deadline for all first quarter away gameplans (through day 22)

·          December 31, 2006 at 10:00pm PST – deadline for all first quarter home game results

·          January 1, 2007 – start of the second quarter

·          January 21, 2007 – deadline for all second quarter away gameplans (through day 44)

·          February 4, 2007 at 10:00pm PST – deadline for all second quarter home game results

·          February 6, 2007 at 9:00pm PST – trading deadline for the season

·          February 7, 2007 – start of the third quarter

·          February 25, 2007 – deadline for all third quarter away gameplans (through day 66)

·          March 11, 2007 at 10:00pm PT – deadline for all third quarter home game results

·          March 12, 2007 – start of the fourth quarter

·          April 1, 2007 – deadline for all fourth quarter away gameplans

·          April 15, 2007 at 10:00pm PT – deadline for all fourth quarter home game results

·          April 18, 2007 – start of round 1 of the playoffs

·          May 14, 2007 – start of round 2 of the playoffs

·          June 1, 2007 – start of round 3 of the playoffs

·          June 27, 2007 – start of the finals

·          July 13, 2007 – finals have been completed, see you all next season!
