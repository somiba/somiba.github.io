2007-08 Season Dates:

 

·          October 13, 2007 – preseason began

·          October 19, 2007 – rookie/free-agent draft lottery resolved

·          October 27, 2007 – expansion draft for new league members

·          October 31, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – each team cut to the roster limit (10, 11, or 12)

·          November 3, 2007 at 8:00am Pacific Time – rookie/free-agent draft

·          November 5, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for picking up players still available

·          November 12, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – trade deadline and deadline for games missed lists, schedule seeding was determined

·          November 14, 2007 – the season has begun

·          Tuesday, December 4, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for first quarter away gameplans through Day 22 on the schedule

·          Tuesday, December 18, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for first quarter home results

·          Wednesday, December 19, 2007 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – trade deadline for the end of the first quarter

·          Monday, January 7, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for second quarter away gameplans through Day 44 on the schedule

·          Thursday, January 24, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for second quarter home results

·          Friday, January 25, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – trade deadline for the season

·          Sunday, February 17, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for third quarter away gameplans through Day 66 on the schedule

·          Sunday, March 2, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for third quarter home results

·          Sunday, March 23, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for fourth quarter away gameplans through Day 88 on the schedule

·          Monday, April 7, 2008 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for fourth quarter home results

·          Thursday, April 10, 2008 – playoffs begin

·          Sunday, April 27, 2008 – second round of the playoffs begin

·          Monday, May 26, 2008 – third round of the playoffs begin

·          Wednesday, June 18, 2008 – final round of the playoffs begin

·          Sunday, July 6, 2008 – playoffs have concluded
