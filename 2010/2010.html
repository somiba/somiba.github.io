2010-11 Season Dates:

 

  • Early October 2010 – Strat releases the cards but not yet the computer game
  • October 15, 2010 – preseason begins, trading is allowed
  • October 17, 2010 – lottery for top 3 picks using online lottery (conditions e-mailed to participants 10/15)
  • October 24, 2010 – card info posted (since the computer game won’t arrive until after the draft)
  • Wednesday October 27, 2010 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – cut deadline to 11 players or 10 players if team made the playoffs last season
  • Saturday October 30, 2010 at 8:00am Pacific Time – annual rookie/free-agent draft
  • Monday November 1 at 10:00pm Pacific Time – supplemental draft (just for leftover players)
  • Sunday November 7 at 4:30pm Pacific Time – expansion draft for new league members
  • Wednesday November 10 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for games missed lists and preseason trade deadline
  • November 11 – start of the season
  • Thursday December 2 at 10:00pm PT – deadline for all first quarter away gameplans through day 22 on the schedule
  • Thursday December 23 at 10:00pm PT – deadline for all first quarter home results (originally 12/16 but several league members had issues getting the gpls in on time or receiving their game
  • Sunday January 9 2011 – deadline for all second quarter away gameplans through day 44 on the schedule
  • Tuesday January 25 2011 at 10:00pm PT – deadline for all second quarter home results and the trade deadline
  • Sunday February 13 2011 – deadline for all third quarter away gameplans through day 66 on the schedule
  • Tuesday March 1 2011 at 9:00pm PT – deadline for all third quarter home results
  • Sunday March 20 2011 – deadline for all fourth quarter away gameplans through day 88 on the schedule
  • Tuesday April 5 2011 at 9:00pm PT – deadline for all fourth quarter home results
  • Sunday April 10 2011 – playoffs round 1 began
  • Wednesday April 27 2011 – playoffs round 2 began
  • Thursday May 19 2011 – playoffs round 3 began
  • Wednesday June 8 2011 – playoff finals began
  • Friday July 1 2011 – finals series ended
