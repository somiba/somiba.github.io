10/31/2004 Rule Changes

  The following rule issues were posted a couple of weeks ago on the

main web page and EC voting was completed today:

1) Use new “emphasize individual defense” option = No by 7-0 vote

     with Ed, Dale, Jeff, Charles, Brian, Jason, and Rick agreeing that

     use of this option would have too many negative effects including

     reducing how often the various results on the defensive card

     (offensive foul, inside block defender, difference between close/sag,

     etc.) come up

2) Roster size modified to 16-18, at least 16 is mandatory with 17 or 18

     optional = Yes by 5-2 vote with Ed, Jeff, Charles, Brian, and Rick

     agreeing that this is a good rule; Dale and Jason chose an option to

     increase all rosters to mandatory 18

3) For any player who played 750 or more NBA minutes increase the

     minutes allowed from 105% to 110% = Yes by 4-3 vote with Ed, Dale,

     Jeff, and Charles agreeing and Brian, Jason, and Rick voting no
