2009-10 Season Dates:

 

·          Mid-October – Strat releases the cards but not the computer game

·          October 24 – draft for new league members

·          October 25 – preseason begins, trading is allowed

·          October 28 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – cut deadline

·          Saturday October 31 at 8:00am Pacific Time – annual rookie/free-agent draft

·          Monday November 2 at 10:00pm Pacific Time – supplemental draft (see far right side of draft link for results)

·          November 30 – Strat releases the computer game

·          December 7 –  preseason trade deadline and deadline for games missed lists

·          December 9 – start of the season

·          December 26, 2009 – deadline for first quarter gameplans through day 22

·          January 10, 2010 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for first quarter game results

·          January 27, 2010 – deadline for second quarter gameplans through day 44

·          February 14, 2010 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for second quarter game results

·          March 7, 2010 – deadline for third quarter gameplans through day 66

·          March 22, 2010 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for third quarter game results

·          April 15, 2010 – deadline for fourth quarter gameplans

·          May 4, 2010 at 9:00pm Pacific Time – deadline for fourth quarter game results

·          May 8, 2010 playoffs began

·          May 25, 2010 second round of the playoffs began

·          June 12, 2010 third round of the playoffs began

·          July 11, 2010 championship series began

·          July 30, 2010 championship series ended
